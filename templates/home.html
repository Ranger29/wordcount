About Me

My name is Miss Adriana Barcinas. I am so excited to teach your child, and I can't wait to get to know each of you! I graduated from Southern Utah University in 2021 with a Bachelor's in Elementary Education, a concentration in Mild-Moderate Special Education, and a minor in Early Childhood Development. I have experience working in religious education settings, elementary schools, daycares, after school programs, and online education

I was Born in Guam, and I grew up in Clinton, UT. I have a wonderful boyfriend named Ranger. I love spending time with Him and with my family, listening to and play music, relaxing in the sun, eating all kind of desserts! My very favorite thing is teaching and giving children a space where they are free to learn, grow, and be happy in the way that is the best for them.

I look forward to learning with you and you child!
